## React State Management

Choosing the right state management library is one of the most critical decisions when building complex front-end applications.

### 1. Redux (Toolkit)
- **Pros**: Predictable, robust DevTools, large ecosystem.
- **Cons**: High boilerplate, steep learning curve.

### 2. Recoil
- **Pros**: Atomic state, React-like syntax, fine-grained updates.
- **Cons**: Slower development updates, experimental APIs.

### 3. Zustand
- **Pros**: Minimal boilerplate, hook-based, superb performance.
- **Cons**: Less structured than Redux.
